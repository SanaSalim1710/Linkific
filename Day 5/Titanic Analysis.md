# Titanic Analysis

## Project Overview

This project used the Titanic dataset and Pandas library to explore the survival patterns and passenger demographics of those onboard the Titanic.
The analysis is based on factors such as age, gender, embarkation port and passenger class.

## Findings 

### Who survived more: men or women?

<img width="571" height="487" alt="Survivors" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/51555ed7-6609-4e97-bdad-7b31b127fb57" />


**~68%** of survivors were women and **~32%** of survivors were men despite men making up majority of the passengers.

Women survived at a much higher rate than men.

<img width="563" height="487" alt="Survival Rate by Sex" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ae33d406-e9ca-4207-9b59-c9b0dfb1e259" />

**~74%** of women survived while only **~19%** of men survived.

This is the likely the result of protocol that prioritised women and children when evacuating on lifeboats.
